    Front design.

    The iconic front in the style of the BMW luxury class inspires with its Iconic Glow illuminated kidney grille and thin daytime running lights typical of M. The distinctive grille with the BMW XM logo and exclusive Night Gold accents exudes luxury and exclusivity. The powerful sculpted bonnet merges smoothly into two power domes. 

    Side view.

    The side view combines the strong character of the design in coup style with powerful proportions and styling highlights such as the darkest windows of the entire BMW model range. Both the 23 light alloy wheels and the accent band that make the shaping of the BMW XM unique create an especially exclusive effect in the accent colour Night Gold.

    Rear design.

    The vertical, hexagonal M twin tailpipes give the rear with gold-coloured diffuser accents and the XM logo a unique presence. The two BMW logos lasered on either side of the rear window make reference to the last M only model the BMW M1. Together with the wheel arches, the surrounding rear lights emphasise the robust, broad stance.

    THE INTERIOR DESIGN OF THE BMW XM.

    Due to regular software updates, screen designs in the communication may differ from the actual screen design in your car.

    Illuminated headliner.

    The indirectly illuminated headliner impresses with a three-dimensional prism structure. Distinctive Ambient light provides a splendid presentation of the various lighting designs such as the welcome scenario. The colour selection can be adapted to individual needs to match driving mode and atmosphere.

    M Lounge.

    The M Lounge offers a unique sensation of spaciousness. The one-piece seating area and details such as the diamond-shaped structures in the headrests and stitching pattern convey comfort and luxury. Together with the sculptural, indirectly illuminated headliner, dark windows and comfort cushions, they create a novel feeling of space.

    Exclusive Vintage leather.

    The leather with vintage character fascinates with its natural structure and texture setting elegant accents in the door trims and instrument panel. This material is being put to use for the very first time exclusively in the BMW XM and the special processing and refinement methods provide unique and individual styling in every vehicle.

    V8-cylinder plug-in hybrid engine.

    The V8-cylinder plug-in hybrid engine stands for pure driving pleasure, superior power delivery and the distinctive M sound. The system power reaches 480 kW and a torque of 800 Nm, all thanks to the virtuoso interaction of the high-performance 4.4-litre V8 and the 145 kW (197 hp)**,**** electrical machine a combination that thrusts the BMW XM forward unbridled.

    Adaptive M suspension Professional.

    The adaptive M suspension Professional impresses with sporty handling without compromising on comfort. The Integral Active Steering stabilises the vehicle at higher speeds and simultaneously reduces the turning radius. Active stabilisers reduce side-to-side body movements, improving driving dynamics and comfort.

    M Sport differential.

    The M Sport differential delivers the best possible performance for your vehicle on the road. It variably distributes the driving power between the rear wheels thus improving traction and driving stability in various situations be it when accelerating out of bends, at high cornering speeds or with differing road conditions.

    COCKPIT AND TECHNOLOGIES.

    Due to regular software updates, screen designs in the communication may differ from the actual screen design in your car.
    • M specific instruments & operating concept
    • Connectivity & digital services

    M specific display concept through BMW Operating System 8.

    The BMW Operating System 8 simplifies vehicle operation, e.g. by voice and touchscreen. The highlight is the M specific design: Content in 2D ensures the best recognisability; the M logo can be found in the presentation of the content. The two M modes ROAD and SPORT create an individual, emotional driving experience.Disclaimer reference invalid

    BMW Curved Display.

    The one-piece BMW Curved Display presents information about the vehicle in a clear manner and over a large area. It combines a 12.3 instrument display with a touch-capable 14.9 Control Display. Operation is ergonomically optimised and intuitive. Its stylish integration into the dashboard also makes the Curved Display a real eye-catcher.Disclaimer reference invalid

    M Setup.

    With M Setup, you can design the driving experience according to your own wishes. Configure driving dynamic parameters according to your individual requirements. The drive, running gear, steering, brake, energy recovery and the all-wheel drive system M xDrive can be adjusted. Retrieve your saved configuration easily using the M1 and M2 buttons on the steering wheel.

    Connected to the Internet.

    Phone and browse in 5G quality when you're out and about. The Personal eSIM connects your BMW to the internet through the external aerials. So now you can browse the internet and stream music or videos conveniently from the comfort of your vehicle. Or use your own smartphone or tablet. Up to ten end devices can be connected online using the built-in Wi-Fi hotspot. Your passengers are sure to thank you for this.Disclaimer reference invalid

    Your key remains in your pocket.

    Your BMW can detect your presence while you are approaching the vehicle and automatically unlock the doors. You can even keep your smartphone or vehicle key in your pocket throughout the process. Share your digital key with up to five people with the BMW Digital Key Plus. The tailgate can be opened by kicking your foot under the rear of the vehicle, while your hands remain free.

    Wireless charging tray.

    More order, more comfort: With the wireless charging tray, you can charge your smartphone wirelessly and thus extremely conveniently. In the extended storage area, there is also room for larger devices within easy reach and without annoying covers and tangled cables. And all this with the greatest possible safety: The built-in cooling protects your device from overheating.

    The BMW Advanced Car Eye 3.0 Pro consists of high-quality QHD and full HD cameras that monitor what is happening in front of and behind the vehicle even when it is parked. If the system senses vibrations and unusual movement outside the vehicle, it automatically stores the event to document possible accidents or (attempted) break-ins.
